Website Launch!.

We are delighted to launch our brand-new IISER Kolkata Movie Club website!

This will be the hub for all things cinema in IISER Kolkata - a place where we host reviews, posters, short films, etc. produced by our community of cinephiles.

This will also be where we keep you updated about upcoming screenings, and news regarding the club and its activities.

Looking forward to a thriving and lively online community!
