Contest Details:

How to Participate:

  • Write your movie review following the guidelines mentioned. You are welcome to embellish the review with pictures and/or art.
  • Submit your review via our content submission Google form - here. Please do not forget to mention in the additional comments section that your entry is meant for the contest.
  • Multiple entries are allowed, and encouraged!

Evaluation Criteria:

  • Originality and creativity
  • Insightfulness and depth of analysis
  • Clarity and coherence
  • Grammar and writing style

We can’t wait to read your insightful reviews and witness the cinematic magic through your words. Whether it’s a timeless classic or a contemporary blockbuster, your unique perspective can shine the spotlight on it. As the cherry on top - all reviews submitted that meet our (not very strict) standards will be published on the Movie Club Website!

Remember that the contest is going to be open till 25th July 2024 - so if you’ve been waiting to see that movie that your friends have been telling you about, go watch it and write a review!
